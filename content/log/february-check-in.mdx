Two things happening in parallel: the cognitive layer in Prism is finally starting to produce useful predictions from event streams, and I've started building this site as a way to make my thinking more visible.

The prediction system uses a sliding window of embedded events to detect pattern shifts. Early results are promising — it caught a deployment-correlated latency spike 12 minutes before the alerting threshold fired. Small sample size but the approach feels right.

This site is intentionally simple. No CMS, no analytics, no comments. Just markdown, a graph, and timestamps.
