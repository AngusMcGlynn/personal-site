This site. Built with Next.js 15, Tailwind, D3.js for the graph, and Velite for content management.

## Why a graph?

Most personal sites are either portfolios (static, performative) or blogs (chronological, disconnected). Neither reflects how thinking actually works — ideas connect laterally, projects inform principles, books reshape how you build things.

The knowledge graph on the homepage makes those connections visible and navigable. It's a map of how things relate, not just a list of what exists.

## Technical choices

- **D3 force simulation** for organic node positioning
- **Velite** for build-time MDX processing with Zod schemas
- **Motion** for UI animations
- SVG over Canvas — ~20 nodes doesn't need Canvas performance
