Most ideas feel clear in your head but fall apart when you try to write them down. That's the point. Writing exposes gaps in understanding that thinking alone can't find.

This site exists because of this principle. The act of writing down what I'm learning, building, and believing forces clarity. The fact that it's public adds useful pressure — I can't hand-wave past the parts I don't actually understand.

Don't wait until you have something polished to say. Write to figure out what you think.
