Large releases are a coordination tax. Every day a branch lives unmerged, it accumulates risk: merge conflicts, stale assumptions, untested interactions.

Ship the smallest thing that teaches you something. A 50-line PR reviewed in 10 minutes beats an 800-line PR reviewed in 3 days, even if the 800-line PR is "more complete."

Revised after building Refract — the tool exists because this principle is hard to follow in practice. Decomposing work into small, coherent chunks is a skill that takes deliberate practice.
